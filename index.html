About Luigi

Luigi is a fictional character featured in video games and related media released by Nintendo. Luigi is portrayed as the younger fraternal twin brother and sidekick of Mario, Nintendo's mascot.

Factos about Luigi

  1. Luigi starred in a game long before Luigi’s Mansion
  2. Luigi was first playable in a 1983 arcade game.
  3. Luigi was actually Dracula’s plumber.
  4. He was originally set to be in Super Mario 64.
